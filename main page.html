Mihajlo Idvorski Pupin

Mihajlo Idvorski Pupin, Ph.D.LL.D.(Serbian Cyrillic: Михајло Идворски Пупин; 9 October 1858 - 12 March 1935),also known as Micheal I. Pupin, was a Serbian American physicist and physical chemist.

Pupin is best known for his numerous patents, including a means of greatly extending the range of long distance telephone communication by placing loading coils (of wire) at predetermined intervals along the transmitting wire (known as "pupinization")

Pupin was a founding member of National Advisory Commitee for Aeronautics (NACA) on March 3, 1915, which later became NASA.
